- The titan of forethought, he created the second race of humans after the first race was wiped out by the battles of the gods
- Stole fire from the heavens to give to mankind, chained to a mountain as punishment where the Kaukasian eagle would tear out his immortal liver each day
- Eventually Heracles freed him.
