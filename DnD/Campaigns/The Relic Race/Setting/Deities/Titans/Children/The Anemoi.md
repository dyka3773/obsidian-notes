The four winds, sons of [[Eos]], some also representing a season:
- [[Boreas]] (North Wind, winter), 
- [[Notus]] (South Wind, autumn),
- [[Zephyr]] (West Wind, spring)
- [[Eurus]] (East Wind, summer)
Sometimes they took the form of horses and pulled [[Zeus]]’ chariot, and many of their offspring are immortal horses.
