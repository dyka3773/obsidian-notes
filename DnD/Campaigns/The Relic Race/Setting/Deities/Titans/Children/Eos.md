- Goddess of the dawn, mother of the four winds ([[The Anemoi]]), daughter of [[Hyperion]] and [[Thia]]
- Her mortal husband [[Tithonus]] shrank into a grasshopper as he aged because Eos only asked [[Zeus]] to grant him eternal youth, but neglected to ask for eternal life
