Symbols: crown, lotus staff, lion

- Goddess of marriage and women, queen of the gods, wife of [[Zeus]]
- Jealous of all of his infidelity (many myths revolve around [[Zeus]]’ attempts to evade her wrath), she managed to conceive two sons (one of them [[Hephaestus]]) by herself.
- [[Zeus]] is the father of her children [[Ares]], [[Eileithyia]] (goddess of childbirth), and [[Hebe]] (goddess of youth)
- She aided some heroes (such as [[Jason]], leader of the Argonauts) and sided with the Greeks in the Trojan War.
