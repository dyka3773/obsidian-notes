Symbols: grain, lotus staff, torch

- Goddess of the harvest, agriculture, and law
- She is a sister of [[Zeus]] and mother of [[Persephone]] (bride of [[Hades]])
- Persephone must spend six months out of the year in [[Hades]]’ realm, and Demeter’s sorrow over her absent daughter causes winter in the mortal world.
