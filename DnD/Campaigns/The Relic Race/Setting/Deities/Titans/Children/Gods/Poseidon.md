Symbols: octopus, trident

- God of the sea, earthquakes, and horses
- He created horses as a gift to [[Demeter]] after several failed experiments such as the hippo, camel, and giraffe
- He is a moody and violent god, prone to lash out with waves or earthquakes
- He is the father of many godlings and water-spirits and a few mortal heroes as well.
