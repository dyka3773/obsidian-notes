Symbols: laurel wreath, bow and arrow, lyre

- God of archery, prophecy, music, and healing, he is an excellent bowman and the brother of [[Artemis]]
- He is sometimes worshipped as the sun god, and his arrows are as piercing as the rays of the sun.
- A lusty god, he chased nymphs, mortal women, and even a few beautiful young men
