Symbols: donkey, hammer, tongs

- The forge and fire god, born lame in one foot (or crippled when thrown from Olympus to the earth by jealous [[Zeus]] or angry [[Hera]]).
- A master craftsman, he and his cyclopes forged [[Zeus]]’ thunderbolts and many of the metal monsters of Greek stories
- He was married to [[Aphrodite]], but divorced her because of her many affairs
