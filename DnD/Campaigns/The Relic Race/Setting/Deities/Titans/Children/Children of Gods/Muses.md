- Minor goddesses of music, arts, literature, and performance
- Their names and domains are:
	- [[Calliope]] (eloquence and epic poetry, she is the mother of [[Orpheus]], the greatest mortal musician in the world)
	- [[Clio]] (historical writing)
	- [[Erato]] (mimicry and erotic poetry)
	- [[Euterpe]] (lyric poetry)
	- [[Melpomene]] (tragic performance)
	- [[Polyhymnia]] (hymns)
	- [[Terpsichore]] (dance and choral song)
	- [[Thalia]] (idyllic poetry and comedic performance)
	- [[Urania]] (astronomical writing)
