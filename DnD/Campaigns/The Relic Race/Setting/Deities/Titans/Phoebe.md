- Titan of answering intellect and the wife of fellow titan [[Coeus]]; together they form the core of all knowledge and discovery in the world.
- She is the mother of [[Leto]], and thus grandmother of [[Apollo]] and [[Artemis]].
- Phoebe is the original owner of the oracle at Delphi, which she gifted to her grandson [[Apollo]]
