# Session 1: The Crown Beneath Thessaloniki

A clean campaign start for [[The Relic Race]] that does not require the one-shot continuity.

## Session Goal
Introduce the world, establish relic stakes, and end with a meaningful choice about [[The Crown of Kings]].

## Premise
The [[Thessaloniki Collegiate of Magic]] hires the party to verify rumors of an ancient relic beneath the city and recover it for secure study.

They are told this is a scholarly retrieval mission.

In the catacombs, they meet [[Nikos - Oathbound Guardian]], who reveals his oath: he guards the Crown from everyone, including states, academies, and cults.

## Start Hook (Academy Commission)

### Quest Giver
[[Professor Helena Markou]] ([[Divination]] Chair at the Thessaloniki Collegiate)
### The Offer
- 40 gp upfront per character
- 60 gp on return
- Formal recommendation letter for future work in the city
- Access to the academy library for one research topic
### Pitch
"A source beneath the old districts suggests a Hellenistic relic chamber exists. We need proof, not plunder. Retrieve records, and if possible, secure the artifact before opportunists do."
## Expected End States (Pick What Happened)

### Ending A: Crown remains with [[Nikos - Oathbound Guardian|Nikos]]
- Party gains [[Nikos - Oathbound Guardian|Nikos]] trust and oath clue
- Academy frustrated but cooperative
- Session 2 hook: political pressure to return with proof

### Ending B: Party secures Crown for academy
- Fast prestige and immediate danger
- Competing groups begin surveillance
- Session 2 hook: escort under threat / possible theft attempt

### Ending C: Crown hidden by party and [[Nikos - Oathbound Guardian|Nikos]] pact
- Maximum agency, maximum complications
- Everyone suspects everyone
- Session 2 hook: [[Dimitris - Information Broker|Dimitris - Information Broker]] offers to sell false leads for coin

## Session 2 Seed Table (d4)
1. Thessaloniki council summons the party
2. Academy rival from Athens arrives with legal claim
3. Cult remnant cell attempts targeted theft
4. Pirate intermediary offers transport to a second relic rumor
