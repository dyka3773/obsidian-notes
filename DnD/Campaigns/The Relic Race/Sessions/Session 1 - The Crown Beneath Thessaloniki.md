# Session 1: The Crown Beneath Thessaloniki

A clean campaign start for [[The Relic Race]] that does not require the one-shot continuity.

## Session Goal
Introduce the world, establish relic stakes, and end with a meaningful choice about [[The Crown of Kings]].

## Premise
The [[Thessaloniki Collegiate of Magic]] hires the party to verify rumors of an ancient relic beneath the city and recover it for secure study.

They are told this is a scholarly retrieval mission.

In the catacombs, they meet [[Nikos - Oathbound Guardian]], who reveals his oath: he guards the Crown from everyone, including states, academies, and cults.

## Start Hook (Academy Commission)

### Quest Giver
[[Professor Helena Markou]] (Divination Chair at the Thessaloniki Collegiate)

### The Offer
- 40 gp upfront per character
- 60 gp on return
- Formal recommendation letter for future work in the city
- Access to the academy library for one research topic

### Pitch
"A source beneath the old districts suggests a Hellenistic relic chamber exists. We need proof, not plunder. Retrieve records, and if possible, secure the artifact before opportunists do."

## Session Flow (3-4h)

### Scene 1: The Briefing (20-30 min)
- Location: Collegiate office
- Tone: scholarly urgency, not panic
- Deliverables to party:
  - old sketch map
  - wax-sealed academy writ
  - warning that city-state agents may also be searching

**Checks**:
- Arcana/History DC 12: The chamber likely predates current Thessaloniki
- Insight DC 12: Markou is withholding one detail (she suspects political interest from city elites)

### Scene 2: Descent and Infiltration (25-35 min)
- Location: [[Location - Thessaloniki Catacombs (Session 1)]]
- Entry: forgotten cistern shaft near the lower city, not through church operations
- Obstacles:
  - unstable floor segment (Dex save DC 12)
  - old ward glyph (Arcana/Religion DC 12 to suppress)
  - 2 sentry cultists scouting (Bandit stats, optional social bypass)

### Scene 3: The Three Doors (30-45 min)
Party chooses one challenge path:
1. Lion Door: stone sentries (combat-forward)
2. Hound Door: Peritas trial (social/skill possible)
3. Serpent Door: Drakofido (hard, puzzle-combat)

Use your existing notes for pacing inspiration, but run only one path.

### Scene 4: Nikos and the Crown (40-60 min)
- Location: relic chamber
- NPC: [[Nikos - Oathbound Guardian]]
- Core tension: custody vs stewardship

Nikos opens with warning, not immediate violence.

**Nikos's stance**:
- "The first tyrant says 'for safety.' The second says 'for order.' The third says 'for justice.' All wear the same face in time."

**Resolution options**:
- Diplomatic: party agrees Crown stays sealed for now
- Conditional trust: Nikos gives proof fragment, not Crown
- Forced conflict: combat, then consequence-heavy outcome

### Scene 5: Exit Pressure (20-30 min)
As party leaves, they encounter signs they were tracked:
- city-state scouts
- one masked relic-buyer envoy
- rumor that "others" now know the chamber is real

End on campaign launch: relic race begins.

## Expected End States (Pick What Happened)

### Ending A: Crown remains with Nikos
- Party gains Nikos trust and oath clue
- Academy frustrated but cooperative
- Session 2 hook: political pressure to return with proof

### Ending B: Party secures Crown for academy
- Fast prestige and immediate danger
- Competing groups begin surveillance
- Session 2 hook: escort under threat / possible theft attempt

### Ending C: Crown hidden by party and Nikos pact
- Maximum agency, maximum complications
- Everyone suspects everyone
- Session 2 hook: Dimitris offers to sell false leads for coin

## Minimal Stat Use
- Scout cultists: Bandit
- Guardian path creatures: use existing references you already prepared
- Nikos: run as a strong veteran guardian with dialogue-first posture

## DM Run Notes
- Keep pace tight: this is Session 1, not lore dump night
- Give 2-3 clear choices at each stage
- Let players talk to Nikos before initiative
- End with one unresolved political consequence

## Session 2 Seed Table (d4)
1. Thessaloniki council summons the party
2. Academy rival from Athens arrives with legal claim
3. Cult remnant cell attempts targeted theft
4. Pirate intermediary offers transport to a second relic rumor
