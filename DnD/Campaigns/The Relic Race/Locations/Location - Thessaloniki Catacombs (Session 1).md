# Location: Thessaloniki Catacombs (Session 1)

A streamlined Session 1 version of the catacombs for [[The Relic Race]], separate from one-shot continuity.

## Purpose
- Fast first-session dungeon flow
- One meaningful branch choice
- Strong social finale with [[Nikos - Oathbound Guardian]]

## Entry Logic
The party does not enter through official church authority.

They descend through a forgotten cistern shaft under the lower city, used long ago to access burial water channels.

## Layout

### 1. Descent Passage
- narrow shaft and old stone stairs
- one hazard (collapse or slippery ledge)
- one ward mark hinting at ancient seals

### 2. Three Doors Junction
Each door represents a different guardian philosophy.

- Lion Door: direct strength test
- Hound Door: loyalty and intention test
- Serpent Door: cunning and coordination test

Party chooses one path only.

### 3. Convergence Hall
A short breathing space after the chosen challenge.

- old inscriptions in Greek and Persian
- signs of old guardian rituals
- foreshadowing of Nikos's oath

### 4. Relic Chamber
- circular chamber
- Crown on pedestal
- low magical pressure in air
- [[Nikos - Oathbound Guardian]] present and alert

## Atmosphere Cues
- damp stone, thin cold air
- distant dripping and echo delay
- old inscriptions carved with intent, not ornament

## DM Notes
- Keep this version lightweight for first-session pacing
- If needed, expand with your one-shot catacombs material later
