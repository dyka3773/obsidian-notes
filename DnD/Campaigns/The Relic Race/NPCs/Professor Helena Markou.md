[[Divination]] specialist at the [[Thessaloniki Collegiate]] and [[Session 1 - The Crown Beneath Thessaloniki|Session 1]] quest sponsor.

## Role in [[Session 1 - The Crown Beneath Thessaloniki|Session 1]]
She commissions the party to investigate relic signs beneath Thessaloniki and recover secure evidence for the academy.

## Why She Hires Adventurers
- academy staff are visible and politically constrained
- she needs deniable field actors
- she suspects rivals are already moving
## Personality
- composed, practical, detail-oriented
- not cruel, but willing to be selective with truth
- prefers evidence over ideology
## What She Gives the Party
- partial map sketch
- academy writ of commission
- upfront pay
## What She Withholds
She does not initially disclose that city elites have already asked the academy to secure relic custody quickly.
## If Confronted About Secrecy
- truthful version: "I needed you focused on retrieval, not politics."
- defensive version: "If I gave full context, you might have sold it before sunrise."
## Campaign Utility After [[Session 1 - The Crown Beneath Thessaloniki|Session 1]]
- recurring employer
- source of research and translation support
- pressure point between scholarship and political power
## Sample Lines
- "Bring me proof first. Possession can wait one day longer."
- "Relics do not remain neutral when states discover them."
- "If this is real, Thessaloniki changes tonight."
