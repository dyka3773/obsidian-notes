# Nikos - Oathbound Guardian

Guardian of [[The Crown of Kings]] in the Session 1 continuity of [[The Relic Race]].

## Core Concept
Nikos is not protecting the Crown from one villain.
He is protecting it from every claimant system: city-states, cults, academies, and conquerors.

## Public Identity
- Ancient-line guardian tied to a long oath
- Lives in the relic chamber complex
- Speaks with restraint and precision

## Motivation
"Power seeks masks: safety, justice, order. The Crown answers all masks the same way."

He believes custody always becomes tyranny over time.

## Conversation Stance
Nikos starts with warning and inquiry, not immediate combat.

### Questions Nikos asks the party
1. Who sent you?
2. What will they do with it when you are gone?
3. If they command armies with it, who stops them?

If answers are thoughtful, he negotiates. If answers are evasive or arrogant, he escalates.

## Resolution Modes

### Mode A: Respectful Withdrawal
Party leaves Crown sealed.

Nikos grants:
- a verifiable relic inscription rubbing
- one truth about the Crown's corruption

### Mode B: Conditional Partnership
Party and Nikos agree to a provisional pact.

Nikos grants:
- access to a proof-fragment or token
- one future audience under strict terms

### Mode C: Hostile Seizure Attempt
If party forces the issue, run combat.
Even in combat Nikos prioritizes defense/disarm over killing.

## Table Portrayal
- calm voice, no theatrics
- no villain monologues
- acts like a man carrying an exhausting burden

## Useful Lines
- "You are not the first good people sent by a bad future."
- "The hand that carries this crown begins by promising restraint."
- "I can trust your intent. I cannot trust your successors."

## Stats Guidance
Use a durable veteran or champion profile appropriate to level 1 party danger tolerance.
Run as dialogue-first encounter.
