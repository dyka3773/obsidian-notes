## Appearance
An older man, but with a well maintained beard and physique. He has a staff at the ready, a large tome under one arm, and wears deep blue and black robes. Hanging at his side are a few vials filled with unknown substances, some of which are glowing. He also has a large bullfrog sitting on his shoulder.
