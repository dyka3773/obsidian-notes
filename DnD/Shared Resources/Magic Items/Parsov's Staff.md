## Description
*requires attunement by a sorcerer, warlock, or wizard*

## Properties & Effects
This staff has 7 charges and regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the staff rots away in your hands. Spells. While holding the staff, you can use an action to expend some of its charges to cast one of the following spells from it, using your spell save DC: [[Ray of Sickness]] (2 charges), [[Blindness Deafness|Blindness/Deafness]] (3 charges), [[Ray of Enfeeblement]] (3 charges).
