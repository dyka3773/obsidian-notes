## Mechanics You'll Reference Often
1. **Attack Roll**: 1d20 + modifier vs. AC
2. **Ability Check**: 1d20 + modifier vs. DC
3. **Saving Throw**: 1d20 + save bonus vs. DC
4. **Damage**: Roll listed dice + modifiers
5. **Advantage/Disadvantage**: Roll 2d20, use high/low

## Useful Mnemonic: SNAFU
When adjudicating something unusual:
- **S**ituation: What's happening?
- **N**eed: Why do they need to roll?
- **A**bility: What ability applies?
- **F**ail/Fortune: What succeeds/fails?
- **U**pdate: Move the story forward
