# Goblin

Standard goblin stat block from the SRD. Use this as a reference or template.

## Stat Block
```statblock
monster: Goblin
```

## Tactical Notes
- **AC**: 15 (leather armor, shield)
- **HP**: 7 (2d6)
- **Speed**: 30 ft.
- **Initiative**: +2 (DEX modifier)

### Combat Behavior
- **Nimble Escape**: Can Disengage or Hide as bonus action
- **Cowardly**: Flees when outnumbered or when leader dies
- **Pack Tactics**: Fights in groups, uses terrain advantages

### Key Attacks
- **Scimitar**: `dice: 1d20+4|Goblin Scimitar Attack` to hit, `dice: 1d6+2|Slashing Damage` damage
- **Shortbow**: `dice: 1d20+4|Goblin Shortbow Attack` to hit, range 80/320, `dice: 1d6+2|Piercing Damage` damage

## Variants
- **Goblin Boss**: CR 1, HP 21, +3 to hit, multiattack
- **Goblin with poison**: Add `dice: 2d4|Poison Damage` on hit (DC 10 CON save)

## Encounter Ideas
- 3-4 goblins for a medium encounter (level 1 party)
- 1 goblin boss + 6 goblins for a hard encounter (level 2 party)
- Goblin ambush on forest road
- Goblin camp raid
